About Us

It began in 1998 when PT. DMSA was first established and registered with its Office In Jakarta Indonesia at Jl. Lodan Raya No.2, Komplek Lodan Center Blok L No.7, Jakarta 14430. Over the past decades PT. DMSA has accumulated a neat and healthy market trust. We have been providing support and services to the National Oil and Gas Company in Indonesia (PT. Pertamina Persero). We supply mostly parts for Turn Around, Services and some other Maintenance Support for Emergency Calls. All our Principals are those that work with in a professional , ethical, competitive and cost efective manner consistent with policies, procedures and business objectives. They all produce goods and services from financial stable, technically qualified and reliable sources.

Since 1999, PT. DMSA has grown gradually mainly providing services for the National Oil and Gas Company. Vendor Registered No. 2186 in PT. Kilang Pertamina International (PT. KPI) and Pertamina Upstream.

  • Thomassen Pumping Unit : PT. DMSA began as a single distributor for the Pumping Unit and Parts for most of the Install Base in Indonesia since 1999 until present time.
  • WIKA Gayesco is one of the leading manufacturers for Pressure Gauges, Temperature Gauges, Thermocouples and Transmitters. PT. DMSA achieved many excellent awards for the prompt delivery and services throughout the years since 2009 until present time.
  • Schneider Electric: PT. DMSA started as a distributor for Field Devices such as Differential Pressure Transmitters and Positioners under the name of Foxboro Invensys before it was later acquired by Schneider Electric. Our Install Base are mostly in Pertamina Refineries.
  • CCC (Compressor Control Corporation) : PT. DMSA were appointed as the Channel Partner in 2019. We had major achievement in Cilacap RDMP RFCC Project and Balikpapan RDMP Project as our largest Install Base in Indonesia. Our Install Base spreads across Oil and Gas Industries, Petrochemical and Fertlizer Industries.
  • PT. DMSA obtained the agency for the Slide Valve Business in 2022 and started with the Turn Around operation in 2024 for PT. Kilang Pertamina Internasional for parts and services of Body Assy., HPCU, Actuator and Check Valve.
